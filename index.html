About

Communication and helping people have been the throughline of my whole career.

Graphic design is how I make ideas clear; accessibility is how I make sure everyone can receive them; and documentation is how I share what I know so others can do the work themselves. For 25+ years at NMSU I’ve built, published, and quality-checked content across a large institutional web portfolio — and I founded Access Forward LLC to bring that same accessible, well-organized approach to other organizations.

I hold an MFA in Graphic Design and certifications including ISC2 CC, with a CPACC accessibility credential in progress. I care about clarity, quality, and lowering the barrier for the next person.

Capabilities

Publishing

Cascade CMS, templated & component-based pages, content collections, publishing governance.

Quality & QA

Staging-to-production review; copy, link, and rendering checks; game & app testing; WCAG/508 auditing with Siteimprove.

Web craft

HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, responsive and semantic/ARIA markup; flagging rendering issues to design & dev.

Knowledge-sharing

Documentation, how-to guides, onboarding, and AI-assisted (Claude) content workflows.
